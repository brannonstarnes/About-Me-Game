All About Brannon...the Game!

My name is Brannon Starnes, I'm 36 years old and currently living in Washington state. I was born in Texas, moved to Japan, Utah, Florida and Tennesse by the time I was in first grade. My family settled down in Tennessee, where I stayed until I was 22, at which point I moved to Salt Lake City, UT to attend the University of Utah.

Speaking of education, I started my freshman year at the University of Tennessee studying Nursing. After working in a hospital for about a year, I realized this was not the field for me. That was the point in my life where I decided to pack it all up and head for Utah, to study and eventually obtain a Bachelor of Science in Adventure and Outdoor Programming, or as my mom would call it, "camping". Although it may sound like my studies included singing "Kumbaya" and making campfires, the degree gave me the skills to teach others how to develop the tools to learn and grow.
